What is Nen?

Nen is the primary power system of Hunter x Hunter, it focuses on focuses ones own life energy to attack and protect themself. Without nen even the most powerful warriors are rendered vulnerable to the weakest nen users. Some humans in hunter hunter are superhuman without nen, but they are few and far between. Still, even them like the likes of Killua would easily be taken out by nen novices.

Nen has four primary principles and many off shoots of them, but these are the beginning. Ten, Zetsu, Ren, and Hatsu, which has it's dedicated page.

Acquiring Nen

Technically speaking, humans don't aquire nen per say, they always have it. Nen is lifeforce and without it everyone would simply die. What's special is being able to visualize, harness, and utilize your natural nen. Across the human body there are dozens of 'nodes' most of these nodes are naturally closed, and there are two ways of opening them.

Natural training

One can naturally awaken and force these nodes open themselves through strenous mental excersise and medidation, the process is long for your average person and might seem trivial, even if the results are more than worth while.

Forced awakenning

Forced awakening is a dangerous prodecre only meant under dire cirumstance. It takes a master of nen to do this process safely, few can. Basically, the master floods the trainee with their own nen, forcibely popping the nodes open. This process is dangerous and has lead to death and worse.

Three founding principles of nen

Ten

Ten is one of the fundamental techniques of Nen, representing a defensive aspect of aura control. When a user employs Ten, they envelop themselves in their aura, forming a protective barrier around their body. Ten is not just a passive defense; it also serves as a foundation for more advanced Nen techniques, allowing users to maintain a constant flow of aura and develop greater control over their energy.

Zetsu

Zetsu is a fundamental technique of Nen that involves the complete suppression of one's aura flow. When a user employs Zetsu, they effectively "shut off" their aura, making it undetectable to other Nen users. Additionally, Zetsu is sometimes employed for meditation and conserving energy, as it requires a heightened state of focus and mental discipline to maintain. However, while Zetsu offers stealth and secrecy, it also leaves the user vulnerable, as they are deprived of the protective aura shield provided by techniques like Ten. Thus, mastering the balance between Zetsu and other Nen techniques is crucial for skilled Nen users in "Hunter x Hunter."

Ren

Ren is a fundamental technique of Nen that involves the amplification and release of one's aura. When a user employs Ren, they emit a significant amount of aura from their body, increasing their physical and Nen abilities. This technique serves as a power-up mechanism, enhancing the user's strength, speed, and durability, making them more formidable in combat.
