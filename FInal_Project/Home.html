What to expect?

The purpose of this site is to take and indepth look into the powersystem of Hunter x Hunter called Nen. It is not for summarizing the actual show itself, rather just the power system within the show. Some spoilers will be included, and it's best to experience within the show itself. However, this site is perfect for HxH watchers who have finished or are well within the show who either need refreshers or are confused about a certain part of Nen.

What is Hunter x Hunter?

Hunter x Hunter is a critically acclaimed manga and anime of author Yoshihiro Togashi, who is also famed for the creation of YuYu Hakusho. It centers around Gon Freeces, and abnormally strong and iron willed 12 year old on his journey to become a Hunter and find his father.
